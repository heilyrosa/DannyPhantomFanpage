Danny Fenton Bio

Daniel "Danny" Fenton, also known by his alias Danny Phantom, is the titular protagonist of the television series. Danny Phantom. Becoming a half-human/half-ghost during a lab accident, Danny has taken on the role of a ghostly superhero to protect Amity Park and the world from the dangerous ghosts of the Ghost Zone.
